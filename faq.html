FAQ

Frequently asked questions about God's Own Country.

  • Why is Kerala called "God's Own Country"?

    Kerala is known as "God's Own Country" because of its lush greenery, serene backwaters, rich culture, and natural beauty that feels almost divine.

  • What is the best time to visit Kerala?

    The best time to visit is between October and March, as the weather is pleasant for sightseeing and activities.

  • What are the top tourist destinations in Kerala?

    Popular destinations include Munnar, Alleppey, Kochi, Wayanad, Thekkady, and Kovalam.

  • What are Kerala’s famous traditional experiences?

    Watching Kathakali performances, enjoying Ayurvedic therapies, houseboat cruises on the backwaters, and savoring traditional Kerala cuisine.

  • Is Kerala safe for tourists?

    Yes, Kerala is considered one of the safest states in India for tourists.
